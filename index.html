Japan has a lot of unique food culture. Let's discover new taste and make your life brighter!

Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese pancake, one of soul-foods in Osaka.

Nikujaga

Nikujaga is a meat stew which is a common home-cooked dish.

Tonkatsu

Tonkatsu is a breaded, deep-fried pork cutlet. It was inspired from french cutlet and adopted into Japanese culinary culture.
